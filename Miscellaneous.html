Miscellaneous

Door knockers are decorative and functional hardware that is mounted on the exterior of doors. They are traditionally used to alert occupants of a visitor at the door by creating a distinctive sound when struck against a strike plate or door knocker plate. Door knockers come in a variety of styles, shapes, and materials, ranging from classic designs to more modern and creative options..


Door stoppers, also known as door stops, are devices attached to the baseboard to prevent a door from swinging open or closing completely. They are designed to create friction or obstruction, holding the door in place. Door stoppers are useful in preventing doors from slamming shut due to drafts, protecting walls, or keeping doors open for convenience, ventilation, or accessibility.


Door domes are architectural elements installed above doors, adding an elegant and welcoming touch to the building's exterior and interior.


Key hooks are small hardware accessories used for organizing and storing keys. They are typically mounted on walls, near entryways or in designated key storage areas.Key hooks provide a convenient and easily accessible place to hang keys, helping to keep them organized and preventing them from getting misplaced.
