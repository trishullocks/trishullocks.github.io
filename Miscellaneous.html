PULL HANDLES

A Pull Handle is a type of hardware commonly used to open Doors.Pull Handles are designed to be grasped and pulled, allowing for easy access to the object they are installed on.

Pull Handles come in various shapes, sizes, and materials to suit different applications and aesthetic preferences. They can be made from metals like Stainless Steel, Brass, or Aluminum.The design of a pull handle can range from simple and functional to intricate and decorative, depending on the intended use and the desired style.

Pull Handles are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. They are commonly found on doors, cabinets, appliances, furniture, and other items where a pulling motion is required to gain access. They provide a convenient and ergonomic way to open or close objects, improving usability and functionality.
