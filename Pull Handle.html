PULL HANDLES

A Pull Handle is a type of hardware commonly used to open Doors. It serves as a means to open or close a door by providing a convenient grip for users.


Door Pull Handles come in various designs, materials, and sizes, allowing for customization and compatibility with different architectural styles and door types. The design of a pull handle can range from simple and functional to intricate and decorative, depending on the intended use and the desired style.


Pull handles are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. They are commonly found on doors, cabinets, appliances, furniture, and other items where a pulling motion is required to gain access. They provide a convenient and ergonomic way to open or close objects, improving usability and functionality.


Beyond its practical purpose, a door pull handle also contributes to the overall aesthetics of a door and its surrounding environment. It can be a decorative element that enhances the visual appeal of the door, adding character and style to both residential and commercial spaces.
