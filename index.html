About Us

TRISHUL


Trishul is a prestigious brand known for its wide range of products, showcased in our brochure for your perusal. Over the past 33 years, the company has experienced significant growth. This growth can be attributed to our continuous focus on technological advancements and improving product quality, which has positioned us as leaders in the industry. Our commitment to maintaining high standards and not taking anything for granted has yielded great results.


At Trishul, we believe in inclusivity and value everyone's input for continuous improvement, leading us towards greater success. The collaborative efforts of our production, research and development, and administration teams have played a pivotal role in achieving this feat.


Last but not least, our Managing Director Mr.Sanjay Agarwal has helped the company to grow at high pace with his experience and foresight. Our well equipped facilities and excellent quality control throughout all stages of production which enable us to satisfy all the needs and maintaining strong bond with the customers.
